Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my Costa Rica trip?

+

For the best availability at luxury accommodations and exclusive experiences, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance. Peak seasons (December-April and July-August) require even earlier planning. However, we can often accommodate shorter notice requests depending on your flexibility.

What's included in a Tlazo bespoke experience?

+

All Tlazo experiences include luxury accommodations, private transportation, expert guides, activities, most meals, and 24/7 concierge support. International flights and personal expenses are typically separate. Each itinerary is customized, so inclusions vary by trip.

Is Costa Rica safe for luxury travelers?

+

Costa Rica is one of Central America's safest countries with a stable democracy and no military. We work only with vetted partners, provide private transportation, and our guides are trained in safety protocols. We also provide 24/7 emergency support throughout your stay.

What's the best time to visit Costa Rica?

+

Costa Rica is beautiful year-round! Dry season (December-April) offers sunny weather perfect for beaches and hiking. Green season (May-November) brings lush landscapes, fewer crowds, and lower prices, with brief afternoon showers. We'll recommend the best timing based on your interests.

How much does a bespoke Costa Rica trip cost?

+

Our bespoke experiences typically range from $800-2,500 per person per day, depending on accommodation level, group size, activities, and season. This includes luxury lodging, private guides, transportation, and most meals. We'll provide a detailed quote based on your specific preferences.

Do you cater to families with children?

+

Absolutely! Costa Rica is fantastic for families. We design age-appropriate activities, select family-friendly accommodations, and can arrange childcare services. Many of our experiences like wildlife viewing, zip-lining, and beach time are perfect for creating lifelong family memories.

What if I need to cancel or change my trip?

+

We understand plans can change. Our cancellation policy varies by timing and components booked. We strongly recommend travel insurance and will help you navigate any changes needed. Our goal is to be as flexible as possible while protecting your investment.

What makes Tlazo different from other tour operators?

Unlike large tour companies, we design every trip from scratch. You have a dedicated travel designer, access to private locations not available to group tours, expert local guides, and luxury accommodations. We're not just tour operators ‚Äì we're your personal Costa Rica concierge.
